# 🌼 BumpBuddy Story Cards
## Version 1.0 (MVP)

> *Every Story Card is a tiny moment of care.*

---

# Purpose

Story Cards define every interaction in BumpBuddy.

Each Story Card represents a complete user journey—from a need, to a support request, to a happy ending.

Every story should be simple enough to complete in under **30 seconds**.

---

# Story Card Template

Every Story Card follows the same format.

## Story Name

### Trigger
What happened?

### Mom State
How is Mom feeling?

### Baby Scene
What is the Dumpling Baby doing?

### Support Request
What should the supporter do?

### Success
How does the story end?

### Assets Needed
Characters, props and effects.

### Animation
What moves?

### Copy
Baby dialogue, CTA and success message.
